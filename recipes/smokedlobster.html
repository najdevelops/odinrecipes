Smoked Lobster

If you’re looking for something to serve for a special occasion, look no further. Lobster is regarded by many as an absolute delicacy. If you want to elevate them even more try these smoked lobster tails.

Ingredients

  • 4oz lobster tails
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • butter
  • garlic
  • juice of a lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat your smoker to 250°F.
  2. Butterfly your lobster tails and pull the meat out of the shell.
  3. Season the lobster meat with salt, pepper
  4. Place the lobster directly on the grates of your smoker with the meat-side facing upwards.
  5. In a small pot or pan, combine the butter, garlic powder, and lemon juice
  6. Place the pot on the smoker next to the lobster. The butter should melt within 5 to 10 minutes, leaving you with a simple butter sauce for basting
  7. Baste the lobster every 10-15 minutes with the butter sauce.
  8. After 45 minutes of cook time, or when the lobster reaches an internal temperature of 145°F, pull the lobster off the smoker and serve alongside the remaining butter sauce and an extra squeeze of lemon juice.
